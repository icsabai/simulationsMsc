Short descriptions

  • Introduce your chosen topic in the following way:
    • describe the problem you chose in some sentences, give the most important equations, figures etc. (topic)
    • introduce it in broader context (background)
    • give reasons why you chose this particular problem (motivation)
    • list the necessary methods and tools you plan to use for the solution (proposed solution)
    • discuss potential error and difficulty sources (obstacles?)
    • describe wat your expected outcomes and results will be, and how you can test their validity, if applicable - e.g. how you can compare your results to literature values or results (hypothesis)
  • Please upload your document in PDF to the szamszim directory on your account!
